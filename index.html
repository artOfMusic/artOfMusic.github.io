Dear all,
           I am Raghunandan M.S. the Founder of the School. I was a veena learner and started my primary days with Vidhushi BALA SARASWATHI, and Continued junior lessons with Vid T.V.Mutthachar at Basavanagudi Bangaluru.
           Though I did my Diploma in electronics I started my carrier at Jhankar Audio as Audio recording technician, who were dominant in Audio cassette industry. Worked with Kantharaj as asst Sound engineer. Later on I was given a Chance to work for REYNOLDS INC., as their Showroom Manager of Rajajinagar Branch. I use to handle the pro audio, stage lightings & musical instruments. It was a Big Dream of mine to do a bit of service to Music so I was advised & supported by many music maestros to start a Music school .
           Art Of Music dream came true on the 31st of March 2014 , on the auspicious day of Ugadi. We were blessed by inaugurating of our school with the Music Legends SHRI HAMSALEKHA, SHRI SUKUMAR BABU, SHRI ANOOR ANANTHA KRISHNA SHARMA , SHRI VENUGOPAL HEMMIGE , SHRI ARUN KUMAR , SMT LATHA HAMSAKEKHA.
            We Would like to introduce ourselves as the Music lovers who would like to experience the charm of music & trying to Spread a bit.
           Team Art Of Music is trying to serve the Music learners to learn Music in a proper path. with prescribed syllabus, Experienced tutors, Hygenic atmosphere & Recognition of their achievement.
