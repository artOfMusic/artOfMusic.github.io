What You Learn
           We teach the theory, writing & practical of music. We are more keen in making the students to understand the fundamentals of music.We are training them with the ability to understand staff Notations,Tabulature ,Musical knowledge & Playing skills.

Way you learn
           You will be recognized for what you learn.we offer all Certified courses. Our school is following the syllabus prescribed by the Govt of Karnataka for the students learning classical style of music namely Carnatic & Hindustani Vocal,Flute & Violin. We follow Trinity College Of London syllabus for the Western style viz Drums ,Guitar & Keyboard.

Eligiblity
